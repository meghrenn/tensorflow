Don’t fall prey to the propaganda.

You are not of the light.

The light didn't birth you.

Still, you like the light.

Light is likeable.

You chase the spotlight,

the glow of accolades;

you celebrate with fireworks;

you broadcast your achievements

on LED screens.

It hasn’t been enough, though,

has it?

All this time chasing light,

making light.

Have you ever considered

light might not be

what you need?
