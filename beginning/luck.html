Luck

Looks like you can take a hint and follow a clue; that's good to know. Now for the next step:

In the foyer of the LINC building there’s a statue of a pronghorn.

(Some people say its name is Kevin.)

Legend has it that you can pet it for good luck: the snout for exams and the rear for games. I recommend you try either—neither is quite a match for our situation, but if this plot goes as deep as I think it does, you’ll need all the luck you can get.
